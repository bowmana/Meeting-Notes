# niche.md

## The Pain (what users complain about)
1. **Manual note-taking during meetings is distracting**
   - Writing notes while listening means you miss things.
2. **Meeting notes are scattered**
   - Some in OneNote, some in Google Docs, some nowhere — no single source of truth.
3. **Action items get lost**
   - "Who was supposed to do that?" — no structured tracking after the meeting ends.
4. **Cloud transcription services raise privacy concerns**
   - Otter.ai, Fireflies, and others upload audio to their servers — not acceptable for sensitive meetings.
5. **Existing tools don't integrate with Notion**
   - Users who live in Notion want notes to land there automatically, not in another app.

## What Meeting Notes Solves
- **Automated transcription** — capture system audio and transcribe without manual typing
- **Structured Notion storage** — notes go directly into a Notion database with proper fields
- **AI-powered summaries** — get a structured overview + extracted action items, generated locally (Private Mode) or via your own cloud API key (API Key Mode)
- **Privacy-first** — audio always stays on your machine. AI runs locally by default. Optional cloud mode puts you in control with your own API key.
- **One-click workflow** — select database, start notes, save to Notion

## What Meeting Notes Does NOT Solve
- Real-time multi-speaker diarization (who said what)
- Video recording or screen capture
- Calendar/scheduling integration
- Replacing Notion as a note-taking platform (we complement it)

## The Niche
**Professionals who attend frequent meetings and use Notion as their workspace:**
- Want automated meeting capture without cloud privacy concerns
- Need structured notes (title, attendees, action items) — not just a text dump
- Already use Notion and want notes to land there directly
- Value local processing over convenience of cloud services
